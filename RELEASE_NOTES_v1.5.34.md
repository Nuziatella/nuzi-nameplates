Gharka Bars v1.5.34

- makes the full custom health bar clickable for targeting
- restores reliable click targeting for normal targetable units, including team member bars
- keeps relation-based bar colors from reverting to the default friendly color
- includes the recent CC and dispellable-highlight improvements

Known limitation:

- click targeting does not currently work for `watchtarget` or target-of-target bars on this client
- those bars still render and update normally, but no API support addon-side targeting path for them
